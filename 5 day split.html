Analysis Modified

The 5 day Modified Split is a mixture of the Upper-Lower and the Push-Pull-Legs splits. It normally incorporates the a half-week of each, separated by a rest day. This split is more advanced than the 3 and 4 day splits, and is great for building muscle and strength. By utilizing this split, you're better able to focus on muscle groups while still maintaining two rest days per week.

This is a great plan for people that spend a lot of time in the gym, but still have time constraints such that working out every day is inconceivable. In my opinion, this is the perfect middle ground split that has a balance between intensity and availability.
