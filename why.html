Why is creating the program the most important part?

Creating the workout program is the most important part of the entire fitness process for a variety of reasons. The most crucial benefit is that it helps you know what to do when you are at the gym (Stanwyck). By having a set plan, you're able to hit the exercises that you know will help you progress. Other benefits include increased longevity of your plan, measured progress, not missing auxilary lifts, and my favorite part, that it's fun to be in control of your life!

It is essential that you target each muscle group two to three times a week for optimal muscle growth. These muscle groups are normally categorized into arms, chest, shoulders, back, and legs. Abdominal movements are not exclusive to one particular group, but rather have the purpose of strengthening your core during each lift. They can also be worked at the end of the session, normally during auxilary exercises.

Having a goal to become more fit is half-baked without a concrete program that helps you to navigate the gym. This is why the different workout programs or splits are crucial for long-term succees in your fitness journey.
