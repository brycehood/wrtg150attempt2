Choosing the best workout split is the most important part of creating a workout plan. Let's find out which one works best for you.

There are many different splits out in the fitness world, but most people don't know which one to pick. Selecting the best split for you is instrumental in your success.

Which one? Full-Body? Upper-Lower? Modified? Push-Pull-Legs?

A workout split is the program that you use when you workout to decide which muscle groups to work when you lift. Some people think it's best to hit every muscle group of your body every time that you lift, but that's not always the best approach! Browse through this website to see which one works best for you!
