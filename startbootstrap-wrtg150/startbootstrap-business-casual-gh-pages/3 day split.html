Analysis Full-Body Split

The Full-Body Split is best for beginners or those that do not have the time to work out every day. This is a 3 day per week split, so every day will require working all the muscle groups. This is a simpler split than the others and ensures that every day that you workout can be maximized for muscle growth throughout your body.

This split is not as intense as the other ones because it only utilizes three of the seven days of the week. Thus, muscle growth is not as apparent or quick. In addition to this, many of the samller muscle groups are neglected because the major muscle groups are the focus on full-body days.
